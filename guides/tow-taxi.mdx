## Overview

The tow and taxi features allow you to provide civilian services within the CAD system. Tow operators respond to vehicle recovery requests, while taxi drivers provide transportation services for citizens.

## Tow Services

### Accessing Tow Interface

<Steps>
  <Step title="Navigate to Tow Page">
    Go to **Tow** from the main navigation menu.
  </Step>
  <Step title="View Active Calls">
    See all active tow requests from citizens and law enforcement.
  </Step>
</Steps>

### Viewing Tow Calls

The tow interface displays all active tow requests with:

- **Caller name** - Who requested the tow
- **Location** - Where the vehicle needs to be towed from
- **Description** - Details about the situation
- **Created time** - When the request was made
- **Status** - Current call status

### Creating a Tow Call

You can create tow calls on behalf of customers:

<Steps>
  <Step title="Click Create Tow Call">
    Click the **Create Tow Call** button.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Enter Call Information">
    Fill in:
    - **Caller name** - Customer or requesting party
    - **Location** - Pickup location
    - **Description** - Vehicle details and reason for tow
    - **Postal code** - If configured
  </Step>
  <Step title="Create Call">
    Click **Create** to add the call to the active list.
  </Step>
</Steps>

### Responding to Tow Calls

<Steps>
  <Step title="Review Call Details">
    Click on a tow call to see full information.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Assign Yourself">
    Click **Assign** to take the call.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Contact Customer">
    Use the caller information to coordinate pickup.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Update Call">
    Add notes about:
    - Estimated arrival time
    - Vehicle condition
    - Destination
    - Any issues encountered
  </Step>
  <Step title="Complete Call">
    Once the tow is complete, click **End Call** or mark it as completed.
  </Step>
</Steps>

### Tow Call Management

When assigned to a call, you can:

- **Update description** - Add notes and status updates
- **Change status** - Update call progress
- **End call** - Complete the service
- **Unassign** - Remove yourself if unable to complete

### Working with Law Enforcement

LEO may request tows for:

- **Impounded vehicles** - Vehicles being taken to impound lot
- **Accident scenes** - Disabled vehicles from crashes
- **Abandoned vehicles** - Vehicles blocking roadways

<Steps>
  <Step title="Receive LEO Request">
    LEO can create tow calls through the dispatch system.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Coordinate with Officer">
    Communicate about:
    - Vehicle location
    - Destination (impound lot, repair shop, etc.)
    - Any special handling needed
  </Step>
  <Step title="Complete Tow">
    Transport the vehicle as instructed.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Confirm with LEO">
    Notify the officer when complete.
  </Step>
</Steps>

### Tow Logs

Track your tow service history:

- View all completed calls
- Review call details and notes
- Track earnings (if configured)
- Monitor response times

## Taxi Services

### Accessing Taxi Interface

<Steps>
  <Step title="Navigate to Taxi Page">
    Go to **Taxi** from the main navigation menu.
  </Step>
  <Step title="View Active Calls">
    See all active taxi requests from citizens.
  </Step>
</Steps>

### Viewing Taxi Calls

The taxi interface shows all active requests with:

- **Passenger name** - Who requested the ride
- **Pickup location** - Where to pick up the passenger
- **Destination** - Where they want to go (if provided)
- **Description** - Additional details or special requests
- **Created time** - When the request was made

### Creating a Taxi Call

Create calls for customers who contact you directly:

<Steps>
  <Step title="Click Create Taxi Call">
    Click the **Create Taxi Call** button.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Enter Ride Information">
    Fill in:
    - **Passenger name**
    - **Pickup location**
    - **Destination** (optional)
    - **Description** - Any special requests or notes
  </Step>
  <Step title="Create Call">
    Click **Create** to add the ride request.
  </Step>
</Steps>

### Accepting Taxi Calls

<Steps>
  <Step title="Review Request">
    Click on a taxi call to see details.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Accept Ride">
    Click **Assign** to accept the fare.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Navigate to Pickup">
    Go to the pickup location specified.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Update Status">
    Add notes when:
    - You're en route to pickup
    - Passenger is picked up
    - You're en route to destination
  </Step>
  <Step title="Complete Ride">
    Mark the call complete when you reach the destination.
  </Step>
</Steps>

### Taxi Call Management

For active rides, you can:

- **Update description** - Add pickup confirmation, destination changes, etc.
- **Modify destination** - If the passenger changes their plans
- **End call** - Complete the ride
- **Unassign** - Cancel if unable to complete

### Best Practices for Taxi Service

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Confirm Pickup Location">

  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Update Status Regularly">

  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Professional Service">
    Provide friendly, professional service to encourage repeat business.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Know the Area">

### Taxi Logs

Track your taxi service history:

- Review completed rides
- View earnings (if configured)
- Monitor ratings (if configured)
- Track service areas

## Call Notifications

Both tow and taxi operators receive notifications for:

- **New calls** - When citizens create requests
- **Call updates** - When details change
- **Call cancellations** - When requests are cancelled

<Tip>
  Enable audio notifications in your browser to hear alerts for new calls.
</Tip>

## Operating Multiple Services

You can operate both tow and taxi services:

1. Access each interface from the respective navigation pages
2. Monitor both types of calls
3. Accept calls based on availability and priority
4. Switch between services as needed

## Citizen Integration

### How Citizens Request Services

Citizens can create tow and taxi calls from their citizen page:

- Click **Create Tow Call** or **Create Taxi Call**
- Enter their information and location
- Wait for an operator to respond

You'll see these requests appear in your active calls list immediately.

### Communication

Coordinate with customers through:

- Call description updates
- In-game communication (voice/text)
- Phone numbers (if provided in call details)

## Dispatch Coordination

If dispatch is active, they can:

- Create tow/taxi calls on behalf of citizens
- Monitor your active calls
- Assign specific calls to you
- Help coordinate multiple operators

<Note>
  Maintain good communication with dispatch for efficient service coordination.
</Note>

## Common Scenarios

### Scenario: Roadside Assistance

1. Receive call for disabled vehicle
2. Confirm location and issue
3. Respond to scene
4. Assess if vehicle needs tow or just assistance
5. Provide service or arrange tow
6. Complete call with notes

### Scenario: Airport Pickup

1. Accept taxi call for airport pickup
2. Confirm terminal and flight details
3. Arrive at designated pickup area
4. Confirm passenger identity
5. Load luggage
6. Transport to destination
7. Complete ride

### Scenario: LEO-Requested Impound

1. Receive tow call from LEO
2. Coordinate with officer on scene
3. Document vehicle condition
4. Secure vehicle properly
5. Transport to impound lot
6. Confirm delivery with LEO
7. Complete paperwork

  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Cannot Assign to Call">
    Ensure the call isn't already assigned to another operator, or check if you have the required permissions.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Call Updates Not Saving">
    Verify you're still assigned to the call and have proper permissions to edit it.
  </Accordion>
