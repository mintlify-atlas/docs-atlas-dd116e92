## Overview

The citizen interface allows you to create and manage citizen characters, register vehicles and weapons, apply for licenses, create businesses, and interact with emergency services.

## Creating a Citizen

<Steps>
  <Step title="Navigate to Citizens">
    Go to the **Citizen** page from the main navigation.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Click Create Citizen">
    Click the **Create Citizen** button.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Enter Basic Information">
    Fill in required details:
    - **First name** and **Last name**
    - **Date of birth**
    - **Gender**
    - **Ethnicity**
  </Step>
  <Step title="Add Physical Details">
    Enter:
    - **Height** and **Weight**
    - **Hair color** and **Eye color**
  </Step>
  <Step title="Enter Contact Information">
    Provide:
    - **Address** (postal, street, city)
    - **Phone number**
    - **Occupation** (optional)
  </Step>
  <Step title="Upload Image">
    Optionally upload a citizen image.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Create Citizen">
    Click **Create** to save your new citizen.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Note>
  Some CADs may require Social Security Numbers or have other required fields configured by administrators.
</Note>

## Managing Citizens

### Viewing Citizen Details

Click on any citizen in your citizen list to view:

- Personal information
- Licenses and their status
- Medical records
- Registered vehicles
- Registered weapons (if enabled)
- Criminal records

### Editing a Citizen

<Steps>
  <Step title="Select Citizen">
    Click on the citizen you want to edit.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Click Edit">
    Click the three-dot menu and select **Edit Citizen**.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Update Information">
    Modify any editable fields.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Save Changes">
    Click **Save** to update the citizen.
  </Step>
</Steps>

### Deleting a Citizen

<Warning>
  Deleting a citizen is permanent and will remove all associated data including vehicles, weapons, and records.
</Warning>

<Steps>
  <Step title="Select Citizen">
    Click on the citizen to delete.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Open Menu">
    Click the three-dot menu in the top-right.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Delete Citizen">
    Select **Delete Citizen** and confirm.
  </Step>
</Steps>

### Marking a Citizen as Deceased

For roleplay purposes, you can mark a citizen as deceased:

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open Citizen Menu">
    Click the three-dot menu on the citizen's page.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Mark Deceased">
    Select **Mark Citizen Deceased**.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Confirm">
    Confirm the action. The citizen will be marked with a date of death.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Licenses

### Viewing License Status

On your citizen's page, the **Licenses** card shows:

- Driver's License (with categories)
- Pilot License
- Water/Boating License
- Weapon/Firearms License
- Fishing License
- Hunting License

Each license displays:
- Current status (valid, suspended, expired, none)
- License number
- Categories (for driver's license)
- Suspension information if applicable

### Managing Licenses

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open Manage Licenses">
    Click **Manage Licenses** on the Licenses card.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Select License Type">
    Choose which license to update.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Choose Status">
    Select the license status from available options.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Save Changes">
    Click **Save** to update your licenses.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Note>
  Depending on CAD configuration, license management may be restricted. Some CADs require license exams or LEO approval.
</Note>

### License Exams

If license exams are enabled:

<Steps>
  <Step title="Request Exam">
    Navigate to the appropriate exam page or contact LEO in-game.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Take Exam">
    Complete the exam administered by a supervising officer.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Receive License">
    If you pass, the license will be automatically added to your citizen.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Vehicle Registration

### Registering a Vehicle

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open Registration Modal">
    Click **Register Vehicle** from the citizen page or vehicles card.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Select Citizen Owner">
    Choose which of your citizens will own the vehicle.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Enter Vehicle Details">
    Fill in:
    - **License plate**
    - **Model** - Select from available vehicle models
    - **Color**
    - **VIN number** (Vehicle Identification Number)
  </Step>
  <Step title="Set Registration Status">
    Choose registration and insurance status.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Upload Image">
    Optionally upload a vehicle image.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Register Vehicle">
    Click **Register** to add the vehicle.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Tip>
  If DMV is enabled, your vehicle registration may need approval from DMV personnel before it's active.
</Tip>

### Managing Vehicles

From the **Vehicles** card on your citizen page, you can:

- **Edit** - Update vehicle information
- **Transfer** - Transfer ownership to another citizen
- **Delete** - Remove the vehicle registration

### Impounded Vehicles

If your vehicle is impounded:

- It will appear grayed out in your vehicles list
- You cannot edit or delete it while impounded
- You must contact LEO to have it released from the impound lot
- Impound fees may apply (depending on CAD configuration)

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open Weapon Registration">
    Click **Register Weapon** from the citizen page.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Select Citizen Owner">
    Choose which citizen will own the weapon.
  </Step>

  </Step>

### Managing Weapons

From the **Weapons** card:

- **Edit** - Update weapon information
- **Delete** - Remove weapon registration

## Medical Records

View your citizen's medical history in the **Medical Records** card:

- Emergency visits
- Doctor appointments
- Treatments received
- Medications prescribed
- Blood type
- Allergies
- Medical conditions

<Note>
  You cannot edit medical records. Only EMS/Fire personnel can create and modify medical records.
</Note>

## Criminal Records

View any criminal history in the **Records** section:

- Arrest reports
- Traffic tickets
- Written warnings
- Active warrants
- Violations and charges
- Jail time served

Each record shows:
- Date and time
- Officer who created the record
- Violations
- Penalties (fines, jail time)
- Case numbers

## Calling 911

Create emergency calls to law enforcement or EMS/Fire:

<Steps>
  <Step title="Click Create 911 Call">
    Click the **Create 911 Call** button on the citizen page.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Enter Call Information">
    Provide:
    - **Location** - Where the emergency is
    - **Description** - Details about the situation
    - **Your name** - Caller identification
  </Step>
  <Step title="Submit Call">
    Click **Create** - Dispatch and units will see your call immediately.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Tip>
  Be specific in your call description to help emergency services respond appropriately.
</Tip>
